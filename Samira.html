Protecting Yourself from AI Voice Cloning

How to Protect Yourself from AI Voice Cloning

To protect ourselves from AI voice cloning, we need to take certain precautions. Here are some key steps you can follow:

1. Verify Caller Identity

  • If you receive a suspicious call or message from an unknown number, verify the caller's identity.
  • Ask them questions that only a real person with past memories would know.

2. Keep a Safe and Unique Password

  • Create a strong and unique password to keep yourself safe.
  • Avoid using easy or common passwords that can be guessed easily.

3. Use Technology Safeguards

  • Use secure and protective websites to protect yourself from cyber threats.
  • Stay aware of potential scams and keep your information private.
